## Pricing conclusion

HexHawk cannot price like a mature signed public reverse-engineering suite until signing, updater, support, and exact-artifact gates are proven. It also cannot compete with free tools on price alone.

The public site now presents:

- Founder Tester: $149 one-time, available by request, unsigned/manual/private delivery.
- Solo Analyst: planned $499/year after signing gate.
- Pro / Lab: planned $1,499/year after signing gate.
- Team / Enterprise: planned from $4,999/year/custom after release-trust gates.

## Trust guardrail

The current purchasable channel is only the unsigned Founder Build. Payment/private distribution is not signed/public release readiness. The website must not expose a normal public checkout or public package download until explicitly approved and release gates are updated.

AETHERFRAME

Give it a rough draft. Get a safer next draft to review.

AETHERFRAME can work with code, documents, plans, or reports. It looks for repeated parts, unclear steps, missing checks, and risky changes. Then it suggests edits and explains what still needs a person to approve.

Reduce

Find repeated text, duplicated code, long steps, or confusing parts and suggest a smaller version.

Reuse

Point to helpers, patterns, tests, or notes that already exist so you do not rebuild the same thing twice.

Review

List what changed, what is risky, what tests are missing, and what a human must check.

EXAMPLE

What it can say.

Input: a messy feature planChecks: repeated work, risky steps, missing testsOutput: a shorter plan with warningsHuman: accepts, edits, or rejects it

People stay in control

AETHERFRAME suggests improvements. It does not secretly ship changes, replace HexHawk verdicts, or promise that every idea is correct.

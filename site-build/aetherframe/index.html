AETHERFRAME

Give it something rough. Get a safer next version to review.

AETHERFRAME is not just a HexHawk feature. It is a standalone improvement workflow: give it code, documentation, a feature plan, or a report; it checks for waste, missing proof, unclear parts, and risky changes; then it returns suggested edits and review notes.

Reduce

Find repeated steps, oversized sections, confusing branches, or unnecessary complexity and suggest a smaller version.

Reuse

Point to existing helpers, patterns, tests, or docs that should be reused instead of reinvented.

Review

Show what changed, what remains risky, what needs testing, and what a human must approve.

People stay in control

AETHERFRAME suggests and packages improvements. It does not silently ship changes, replace HexHawk verdict authority, or guarantee correctness.

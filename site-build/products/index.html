BUYING GUIDE

Choose by job, not by acronym.

HexHawk is for local binary evidence and report handoff. AETHERFRAME is for bounded improvement/review. AetherFrameGuard is for careful CS2/PC measurement. Hermes Access is owner-only operational material. Keeping those jobs separate makes the site easier to trust.

POSITIONING

Where HexHawk fits.

Established reversing products emphasize disassembly, decompilation, graphs, debugging, scripting, plugins, and documentation. Sandbox services emphasize safe execution environments and behavior reports. HexHawk should not blur those categories: it is a local evidence workbench, and STRIKE organizes imported runtime evidence rather than turning HexHawk into a cloud sandbox.

Simple promise
